02. Impact Pillars

Hover over the cards to explore how educational lag restricts regional development.

Regional Economy

How does education affect local GDP and labor?

The lack of learning in villages limits innovation and job opportunities, causing forced migration and long-term economic stagnation.

Governance

Can a democracy function without education?

A lack of "voting education" leads to unbalanced national decisions, as citizens may lack the tools to evaluate political choices effectively.

Mayan Identity

The risk of linguistic erosion.

Without formal bilingual education, youth prioritize Spanish for survival, causing ancestral languages to disappear within generations.

03. Project Process & Results

The Final Objective

The result of this project is to create awareness about the economic disadvantage. Through the help of an informative video, the audience will be more informed about how it impacts communities and curtails development opportunities. Our aim is to invite viewers to ponder realistic solutions that lead to a more informed and responsible audience.

The Methodology

The process started with identifying a relevant topic. We went through a brainstorming process to discuss affected groups and specific causes. This allowed us to contemplate which root issues are most important to address so the problem can be reduced effectively.

Technical Execution

To record our informative video, we utilized effective materials: a tripod for stability and a high-quality microphone for audio clarity. The project was edited using specialized software like Filmora, Canva, and CapCut, ensuring credible data was presented through valid research sources.
